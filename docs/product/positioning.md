# TEAmate — Positioning

## One-liner
**TEAmate is an agentic meeting companion that keeps meetings on agenda and turns talk into trackable work.**

## Elevator pitch
Meetings are where work is supposed to align—yet they often drift, end without clear next steps, and force teams to rehash context every week.

TEAmate sits alongside your meetings as a proactive companion: it detects when the conversation drifts away from the agenda, captures decisions and action items, and converts them into tickets that match your existing workflow. Over time, TEAmate becomes a living meeting memory—so recurring meetings build momentum instead of restarting from zero.

## Target users
- Meeting organizers / facilitators (PMs, team leads)
- Teams with recurring cadence meetings (weekly planning, status, cross-functional syncs)
- Roles who benefit from agentic workflows but need simple UX and integrations

## Key promises
- **Stay on track:** drift detection + nudges to return to agenda.
- **No lost outcomes:** action items become tickets/tasks.
- **Never lose context:** meeting memory across a series.

## Why now
- Teams already have transcripts and copilots, but the “last mile” is missing:
  - steering the meeting in the moment,
  - integrating outputs into work systems,
  - continuity across meetings.

## Alternatives and our angle
- Generic copilots / chat-based summarizers: great at summarizing, weaker at workflow execution and meeting continuity.
- Meeting note apps: great at capture, weaker at agency and conversion into real work.

TEAmate competes by combining:
1) **meeting-time agency**,
2) **workflow integration**,
3) **longitudinal memory**.
